Web dashboard

Build booth events from any browser.

The dashboard centralizes event setup, QR links, booth types, gallery settings, and uploaded photo management.

  • Event QR and gallery links
  • Physical and virtual booth setup
  • Gallery branding, downloads, and moderation
Smart templates

Apply the right frame automatically.

When a booth captures a photo, PoloPro can detect its orientation and use the matching portrait or landscape template.

  • Orientation-based template selection
  • Portrait and landscape frames in one event
  • Frame assets synced with booth setup

Core capabilities.

QR sharing
